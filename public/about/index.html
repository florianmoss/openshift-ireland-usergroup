Mission Statement

The OpenShift Ireland User Group is mainly aimed at existing OpenShift users in Ireland but is also open to professionals that are looking to find out more about OpenShift and to learn from user experiences. It is meant to be a platform for knowledge transfer and exchange of experiences. The user group will focus on presenting and discussing real workloads that customers are running and have customer presentations focus on such.
